Haven

A discreet layer of protection

Haven is a personal safety app that lets you silently alert a trusted contact in an emergency, including from your lock screen via Siri, without unlocking your phone. One voice command is all it takes.

How it works

Simple by design. Ready when you need it.

Haven is designed to work in moments where speed and discretion matter most.

1

Set up a trusted contact

Choose one person you trust. They will receive your alerts with your location when you need help.

2

Say the word

Use a single Siri voice command from your lock screen. No need to unlock your phone or open the app.

3

Your contact is alerted

Your trusted contact receives an instant notification with your GPS location. Silently. Discreetly.

For institutions

A duty-of-care solution for schools, care homes, and organisations

Haven is preparing a free pilot programme for September 2026. We are actively partnering with institutions across the Cayman Islands.

Schools

Recommend Haven to parents as a safety tool students can use outside of school hours. A discreet way for young people to reach home when they need to.

Care providers

Provide residents and staff with a simple emergency tool that works without drawing attention. Suitable for vulnerable adults and supported living environments.

Organisations

Offer Haven as part of your employee safety or community support initiatives. A practical, low-cost addition to your duty-of-care framework.
