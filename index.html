Haven

A discreet layer of protection

Haven is a personal safety app that lets you silently alert a trusted contact in an emergency — including from your lock screen via Siri, without unlocking your phone. One voice command is all it takes.

Available on iOS Android coming soon Built in the Cayman Islands

How it works

Simple by design. Ready when you need it.

Haven is designed to work in moments where speed and discretion matter most.

1

Set up a trusted contact

Add the person you trust most. They will receive your alerts instantly when you need help.

2

Say the word

A single Siri voice command from your lock screen activates Haven. No need to unlock your phone or open the app.

3

Your contact is alerted

Your trusted contact receives an instant notification. When the app is open, your GPS location is included automatically.

Community Impact Award Winner, BDC 2026

Haven was recognised at the 2026 Cayman Islands Business Design Competition for its potential to support vulnerable individuals in the community. The award reflects the genuine need for simple, accessible personal safety tools in everyday life.

For institutions

A duty-of-care solution for schools, care homes, and organisations

Haven is preparing a free pilot programme for September 2026. We are actively partnering with institutions across the Cayman Islands.

Schools

Something parents can set up on their child's phone. If a student ever feels unsafe outside of school, one voice command silently reaches a parent or trusted adult.

Care providers

A simple safety tool for caregivers and the people they look after. Designed to be easy to use for people of all ages and abilities.

Organisations

For any organisation where staff or members work alone, travel, or operate in environments where personal safety is a consideration.
